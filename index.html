Synthetic Beef from Navel Bacteria?

Impossible Beef is conducting research to find the best tasing synthetic beef. They hope to find a bacteria that will suit their needs, and believe it may be found in an unlikely place: someone's belly button. Using the data collected we've prepared the charts below to visualize the kinds of bacteria found in research subjects navels. We've identified the types of bacteria found in their navels as well as the top ten bacteria found and how often the subjects scrub their navels each week.
